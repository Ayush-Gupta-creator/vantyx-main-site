FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take? +

5 to 7 working days for most projects. We deliver fast without compromising quality.

What's the difference between the AI Chatbot and Automation System? +

The chatbot handles conversations with customers. Automation Systems go further — they run your backend processes too, like membership reminders, follow-ups, and daily reports, with no one manually sending a message.

How does payment work? +

50% advance before work starts, 50% on delivery. Payment via UPI. Simple and transparent.

Can I update the website later? +

Yes — every project includes a free revision round. For ongoing updates, our Monthly Retainer plan covers you.

Do you work outside Sonipat? +

Absolutely. We work with businesses across Haryana — Panipat, Karnal, Ambala, Faridabad, Gurugram — and beyond. Everything is handled online.

What if I'm not satisfied? +

We work with you until you're happy. Revisions are included in every plan, and we don't consider a project done until you approve it.
